Technical Appendix

SARIMA (Seasonal Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average) is a popular statistical model used for time series forecasting, particularly when data exhibits seasonal patterns. It extends the ARIMA model by incorporating seasonality, making it suited for datasets with recurring trends, such as CSM's typical seasonal fluctuations.

The SARIMA model used for forecasting was developed in Python, leveraging the statsmodels library. The full code, including model tuning, validation, and visualization, is available in the project repository. You can access it here to explore the methodology behind the projections.
